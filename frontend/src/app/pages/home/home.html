Our Story

Why Choose Shiv Agri Consultancy

"From Soil to Soul — Building a Greener Tomorrow."

25 Years of Walking the Fields

For over 25 years, I have walked through the fields of Gujarat — from the fertile plains to the dry lands, from lush orchards to newly sown farms. There are few places where my footsteps haven't touched the soil, where I haven't listened to the silent stories of the earth.

"I am an Agronomist by profession, but a farmer by heart — someone who believes that every leaf, every root, and every grain carries the rhythm of life itself."

Nature as Our Teacher

Nature has been my greatest teacher, and the land my lifelong companion. Working with farmers, guiding them, and watching barren fields turn into thriving farms has been not just my work — it has been my blessing.

Through Shiv Agri Consultancy, I bring together science, experience, and emotion — blending modern agricultural technology with the timeless wisdom of farming.

Building Lasting Relationships

At Shiv Agri Consultancy, we don't just offer services — we build relationships that grow like roots, deep and lasting. Your land becomes our land. Your success becomes our pride.

Together, let's create a future where the soil breathes again, where our children inherit not just farms, but true, living lands.

Comprehensive Services

From soil testing to farm management, landscaping to fruit crop consultancy, my mission is to nurture every inch of land with care, knowledge, and vision.

Let's turn every field into a story of growth, every harvest into a celebration — and make your land a heaven that holds your family's memories for generations to come.

Your Land. My Knowledge.

One purpose — a greener, brighter future.

— Anil Hadavani (M.Sc. Agri.)
